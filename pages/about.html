After 15 years in the high-pressure world of emergency healthcare, I decided it was time for a creative adrenaline rush of a different kind—graphic design!

I recently completed my studies in design, where I developed skills in branding, visual communication, infographics, and editorial design. Along the way, I stumbled upon the fascinating world of UX/UI, which instantly hooked me—so much so that I’ve decided to keep leveling up my skills in this field.

My approach is a mix of creativity and precision, always with an eye for detail and functionality. I love crafting designs that not only look great but also connect with people in meaningful ways.

Now, I’m ready for new challenges, exciting collaborations, and bold creative adventures. Let’s make something amazing together!
