Explore the project on Behance

Project

Bachelor’s Final Project in Design and Digital Creation

Date

2024

Overview

Interaction design project focuses on the creation of a mobile application for artists, art lovers and professionals in the sector, using User-Centered Design ( UCD) and esign Thinking methodologies.

Role

My role in this project covered all stages of a comprehensive UX/UI interaction design process. It included the early research phases, where I worked on modeling and conceptualization using tools such as Personas, empathy maps, scenarios, and requirement gathering. I then moved on to defining the information architecture and designing the user experience through card sorting, content tree structuring, flow diagrams, wireframes, and initial user testing. Finally, I designed the user interface, creating a high-fidelity prototype and developing the product’s visual identity.

Challenge

Many emerging artists face significant challenges at the beginning of their careers. Showcasing their work, building a professional network, and finding spaces to exhibit or sell their art can be a difficult path. The lack of visibility and accessible platforms limits their opportunities for growth and recognition in the art world.

Solution

The solution to this problem is ArtHub, an app designed to bring together artists from various disciplines in one place. This platform will allow users to showcase their work, sell their pieces, and connect with other professionals in the field. ArtHub presents itself as an all-in-one space that aims to stand out from other existing platforms by offering a more complete, accessible experience focused on the real needs of artists.
