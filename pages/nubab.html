BRANDING

NUBAB, visual identity

Explore the project on Behance

Project

Project I/UOC

Date

2021

Challenge

Design the visual identity from scratch for a Pop Up Store, a themed, temporary, and ephemeral retail space dedicated to exclusive, self-produced products. The challenge was to create an attractive proposal that would convey the brand’s values and connect with an audience that values originality and sustainability.

Role

Responsible for the comprehensive development of the visual identity for the Pop Up Store, including brand conceptualization, definition of its value proposition, and creation of all graphic elements. The work ranged from research and reference analysis to the final design of the logo, color palette, typography, and graphic applications adapted to the retail space.

Solution

Creation of the visual identity for NUBAB, a sustainable fashion brand with local production and limited editions. The design, inspired by nature, conveys the brand’s values and adapts to the ephemeral format of the Pop Up Store, achieving a coherent, attractive, and memorable image.
